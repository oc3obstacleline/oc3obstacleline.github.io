About the Music

In 1783, Boccherini composed a Christmas Pastorale for a Villancico, a typically Spanish genre. He was Italian, but lived in Spain and integrated the music of his host country in a remarkable way. Shortly before his death in 1801, he revived this music in a string quintet that has never been recorded
